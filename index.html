I ,mostly, am a rock person and if youre also someone who is also into rock music, check out this cover band called First To Eleven. They’re pretty cool.

Another band worth mentioning is Polyphia. They’re an instrumental progressive rock band whose style falls somewhere between blisteringly fast metal-god virtuosity and pure pop. Well, to be honest, you cant really classify polyphia into a genre. In many ways, it’s this lack of classification that has catapulted them to the top of the instrumental metal scene. There is no one quite like Polyphia.
